A few days ago, I delivered an oral presentation at ICASAT 2025 in Querétaro regarding a paper accepted for publication in the conference proceedings by Springer, in addition to presenting a scientific poster.

I am deeply grateful for the support of Dr. Delfino Vargas from PUED, Dr. Pablo Vera, and Ph.D. candidate Marivel Zea from CICATA for their collaboration on this project. 

**Participation details:**
* **Oral presentation:** *"Exploring and Evaluating Multisource Remote Sensing for Poverty Mapping in Mexico"* (Technical Session 4).
* **Scientific poster:** *"Evaluating CNN Architectures for Poverty Prediction in Mexico"*.
* International Conference on Aerospace Science and Advanced Technology (ICASAT). October 2, 2025, Querétaro, Mexico.
